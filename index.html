Our Story

Soulmates are truely made in heaven! And you will meet one when the time is right. Like we met each other. We both were introduced by our common friend back in Pune,2019 and after lot of persuasion, we decided to contact each other. Initially we limited our interactions to virtual world. We got to know each other a little better with every passing day. And we decided to meet face to face.It was casual meet but lead to second meet and that to a trip to lonavala. At that moment we were friends nothing more nothing less and was going with a flow. We liked each other's company. But in every smooth ride there is a bump and so was in ours. When I(Babneet) decided to leave Pune and move to Gurgaon, Probably that was our last meet. We continue to chit chat with each other and it continued for months. During this time our connection brewed and finally we both took first step for our long term entanglement, Journey to forever.
